Dashboard-Payzant Home Hardware

Abstract

The aim of the Project is to manage the inventory for Payzant Home Hardware and to help predict the quantity of products required to order and when to make an order. The limited data provided led us to thoroughly clean the data and take out key data points to help with the prediction of daily demand. The project also focuses on a metric called the risk factor which uses the present Quantity on Hand (QOH) to determine the need to build up the inventory.

The Project followed an Agile Methodology and started with Business Analysis of the requirements, formulating the Key Performance Indicators (KPI) and designing the analysis and prediction models around those KPI’s and finally using the UI to form the Dashboard to better portray the predictions and analysis for management of the Inventory.
